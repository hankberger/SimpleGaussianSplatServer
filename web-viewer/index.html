Controls

Mouse

Left drag — Orbit
Right drag — Pan
Scroll — Dolly in/out
Ctrl+Scroll — Orbit
Shift+Scroll — Pan

Keyboard

Arrows — Move
Shift+Arrows — Move up/down
WASD — Look
IJKL — Orbit
QE — Roll
P — Carousel
V — Save view

Touch

1 finger — Orbit  |  2 fingers — Pan, pinch to zoom, rotate
